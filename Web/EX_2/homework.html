Hollow Knight

Hollow knight is a 2017 action-adventure metroidvania video game. It was developed and published by Team Cherry. The game follows a nameless knight, known as a "vessel" or “ghost”, as they traverse an ancient plague-infested kingdom inhabited by various insects, known as Hallownest. The Knight must travel through Hallownest, fighting bosses and unlocking new abilities to progress, as they uncover the mysteries of the kingdom.
